The app may store information you enter, including workout names, exercise names, sets, reps, weights, exercise notes, workout dates, cardio mileage, run times, shoe names, shoe mileage, account email address, and related fitness log information.

The app may use Firebase Authentication to support guest access, account sign-up, and login. If you create an account, your email address and authentication identifiers may be processed by Firebase to provide account access and cloud sync.

The app may use Firebase Cloud Firestore to save and sync workout data. Cloud sync is used to help preserve workout history and make app data available after reinstalling or signing in.

Workout does not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers used to operate the app, including Firebase and advertising services when enabled.
