Delete Your Bar Life Account

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Bar Life accounts are optional and are used for cloud backup and restore. You may request deletion of your Bar Life account and the associated cloud backup data at any time.

Request account deletion

How to request deletion

  1. Select Request account deletion above.
  2. Send the prefilled email from the email address associated with your Bar Life account, or include that account email in the message.
  3. Use the subject line “Bar Life Account Deletion Request.”
  4. We may contact you to confirm the request before deletion is completed.

What is deleted

After a verified deletion request, we delete the Bar Life account and cloud-backed app data associated with that account, including cloud-stored shifts, earnings, venues, coworkers, events, notes, settings, and reminder preferences.

What may remain

Data stored only on your device is not removed by an email request. You can remove local data by deleting it in the app, clearing the app’s storage, or uninstalling Bar Life from your device.

Limited information may remain temporarily in secure system backups or logs for up to 30 days, where needed for security, fraud prevention, legal obligations, or reliable service operation. Such information is not used to restore your account after deletion.

Timing

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days.
