Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Bar Life is a shift, tip, and earnings tracker for hospitality workers. This Privacy Policy explains what information Bar Life handles, why it is handled, and the choices available to you.

By using Bar Life, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information Bar Life Handles

Shift and earnings information

Bar Life stores information you enter, such as shift dates, workplaces, roles, hours, hourly rate, cash tips, card tips, coworkers, who closed, events, notes, and reminder preferences.

Account information

If you create an account or sign in, Bar Life uses Firebase Authentication to handle your sign-in information, such as your email address and password. Bar Life does not receive or store your password directly.

Cloud backup information

If you sign in and use cloud backup, your app data is stored in Firebase services associated with your account so it can be restored on another device. Cloud backup may include the shift, venue, crew, event, settings, and reminder information you choose to save in the app.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Bar Life asks for notification permission and stores reminder settings on your device. Notifications are used only to remind you to log shifts. You can turn off notifications in the app or in your device settings.

How Information Is Used

Bar Life uses the information described above to:

  • save and display your shifts, earnings, reports, trends, and insights;
  • provide venue, role, crew, event, and historical comparisons;
  • send shift reminders when you enable them;
  • provide account sign-in and cloud backup/restore features; and
  • maintain and improve app functionality and reliability.

Data Storage and Security

Bar Life is designed to work locally on your device. If you do not create an account or use cloud backup, your data stays on your device.

When you choose to sign in and back up data, Bar Life uses Firebase services provided by Google. Data is transmitted using encrypted connections supported by those services. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and Bar Life cannot guarantee absolute security.

Sharing of Information

Bar Life does not sell your personal information. Bar Life does not share your shift or earnings data with advertisers.

Information may be processed by service providers needed to operate app features, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore for sign-in and optional cloud backup.

Your Choices and Controls

  • You can use Bar Life without creating an account.
  • You can choose whether to enable notifications.
  • You can edit or delete shifts, venues, crew, events, and notes in the app.
  • You can sign out of your account from the app.
  • You can contact us to request help with account-related deletion questions.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. Cloud backup data remains associated with your signed-in account until it is replaced, deleted through available app features, or removed following an account-related deletion request.

Children's Privacy

Bar Life is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Bar Life changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
