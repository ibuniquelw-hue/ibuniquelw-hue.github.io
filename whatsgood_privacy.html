This Privacy Policy explains how What's Good, published by Twenty Over All LLC, collects, uses, and protects information when you use the What's Good mobile app.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect and process:

  • Account information such as your email address for login and authentication
  • Restaurant notes, food ratings, people profiles, group information, and saved dining preferences you create in the app
  • Location information when you use nearby restaurant or map-based features
  • Basic device and app usage information needed for functionality, performance, and troubleshooting
  • Advertising-related data through third-party ad services if ads are enabled

How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide login, syncing, shared group features, and core app functionality
  • Store and display your restaurants, food notes, ratings, and people preferences
  • Help you discover nearby restaurants and open map/navigation features
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Support advertising or monetization features if enabled

Location

What's Good may request location access when you use nearby restaurant discovery. Location is used to find restaurants near you and is not required for basic saved restaurant and food note features.

Advertising

What's Good may display ads through third-party advertising providers, including Google AdMob. These providers may collect device identifiers, usage data, and related information to serve and measure ads in accordance with their own privacy practices.

Data Storage

Your information may be stored locally on your device and may also be stored in cloud services used to support authentication, syncing, group sharing, and app functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only as needed to operate the app, such as authentication, cloud storage, maps/location services, analytics, and advertising services. If you use group features, restaurant notes and preferences may be visible to members of your shared group.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide app functionality and comply with legal obligations. You may remove content you create in the app at any time through available app features.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Edit or delete restaurants, food notes, people, and saved preferences within the app
  • Choose whether to sign in or use cloud sync features
  • Choose whether to grant location permission for nearby restaurant features
  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Contact us with questions about your data

Children's Privacy

What's Good is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at toa.apps@outlook.com.
